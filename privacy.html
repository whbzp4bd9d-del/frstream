Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20th July, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Front Row Stream ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our website (frontrowstream.live).

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user registration or collect personal information like names or payment details. However, like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-personal information when you visit, including:

  • Log Data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited.
  • Device Information: General information about the device you use to access the Site.
  • Cookies: We may use essential cookies to maintain site functionality and analytics cookies to understand site traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to operate, maintain, and improve the performance of the Site, analyze usage trends, and monitor for fraudulent or abusive activity.

3. Third-Party Services and Embedded Content

When you click on a "Watch" button or interact with an embedded stream, you are interacting with a third-party service provider. We do not control the privacy practices, cookies, or advertisements of these third-party sites. Third-party providers may collect your IP address, use tracking pixels, or serve targeted advertisements. Your interaction with third-party sites is governed entirely by their respective Privacy Policies, not ours.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our analytics partners. We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Since we only collect anonymized log data, you can typically exercise these rights by clearing your browser cookies or contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@frontrowstream.live
